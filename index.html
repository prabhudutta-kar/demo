About Me

Front-End developer | UI/UX designer | Coder

Hi there! I’m a front-end developer who loves to code. I strive to create simple, original and impactful user experiences. I take on projects of all sizes and spend most of my work day building Web applications. I primarily work in jQuery, vanilla JavaScript, Bootstrap, HTML5/CSS3. I think in design just as much as code, and I find a way to keep a balance between design and development — even if that's just working on some art or design projects on the side.

I now work remotely as a freelancer Web designer and developer. I've designed and built many sites alone from start to finish. But it's only so much fun to be a one-man band. There's nothing better than riffing ideas around a team you love to work with and sharing the honor of a job well done.
